Flights Tableau Project

The Tableau Visualisation is compiled from self scraped and cleaned UK flight data spanning from July 2019 to February 2021. The visualisation shows specific airport activity, and is compared to the same period that time last year. You can clearly compare airport performance during covid and look at popular international countries that are being travelled to recently, as travel rules relax.

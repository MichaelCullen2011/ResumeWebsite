PROJECTS

Neural Networks and Machine Learning

The two projects here explore neural style transfer networks and a text based recurrent neural network. The NST network uses a style image (usually an artists painting) which is fed through the network and blended onto a second image, the content image of your choosing. The text based RNN learns from a specific text; such as Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet or the Harry Potter books, and then generates characters (letters, spaces, punctuation) to imitate these styles of writing.

Games

The two games; Chess and Goban, are programmed using the popular pygame library. Each game has been programmed such that two players can play each other from the same computer. Chess also allows for various technical chess rules such as castling and pawn enpassant captures. The Goban game is based on China's Go board game, where players compete for territory using coloured stones.

Physics Simulations

The two simulations presented are a simple solar system motion simulation in vpython, a neutrino probability calculator used in my University Masters project, and various quantum simulations. Interstellar Motion has all of the solar system planets programmed in with the focus being on accurate time periods. The Quantum and Neutrino simulations calculate either the probabilities of a neutrino and lepton exchanging bosons or the electron density of hydrogen orbitals.
